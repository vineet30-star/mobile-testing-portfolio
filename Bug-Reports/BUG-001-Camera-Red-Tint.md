BUG-001: Camera Red Tint Issue

Company

Xiaomi (POCO)

Device

POCO X7 Pro

OS Version

HyperOS 3.0.5.0

Summary

Human skin tones appear reddish in photos after updating to HyperOS 3.0.5.0.

Steps to Reproduce

1. Open Camera App
2. Capture photo of a person in daylight
3. View captured image

Expected Result

Skin tones should appear natural.

Actual Result

Skin tones appear red or warmer than actual.

Medium

Priority

High
