BUG-003: Excessive Device Heating During Charging After HyperOS 3.0.5.0 Update

Company

Xiaomi (POCO)

Device

POCO X7 Pro

Previous Version

HyperOS 2.0

Current Version

HyperOS 3.0.5.0

Summary

Device becomes noticeably warmer during charging after updating to HyperOS 3.0.5.0. The issue was not observed on HyperOS 2.0.

Description

After updating to HyperOS 3.0.5.0, the device temperature increases significantly while charging using the original charger and cable. Users may feel excessive warmth on the back panel during charging.

Steps to Reproduce

1. Connect the original charger and cable.
2. Start charging the device.
3. Leave the device charging for 15–30 minutes.
4. Monitor device temperature during charging.

Expected Result

Device temperature should remain within normal operating limits while charging.

Actual Result

Device becomes noticeably warmer than before during charging.

Severity

Medium

Priority

Medium

Status

Open

Environment

- Device: POCO X7 Pro
- OS Version: HyperOS 3.0.5.0

User Impact

Users may feel uncomfortable while charging the device and may have concerns regarding battery health and long-term performance.

Notes

The issue was not observed on HyperOS 2.0 under similar charging conditions.
