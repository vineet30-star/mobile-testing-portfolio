BUG-002: Battery Optimization Performance Degradation After HyperOS 3.0.5.0 Update

Company

Xiaomi (POCO)

Device

POCO X7 Pro

Previous Version

HyperOS 2.0

Current Version

HyperOS 3.0.5.0

Summary

Battery optimization performance appears to be less effective in HyperOS 3.0.5.0 compared to HyperOS 2.0.

Description

After updating to HyperOS 3.0.5.0, users may observe increased battery consumption during daily usage. Battery backup appears lower compared to the previous HyperOS 2.0 version under similar usage conditions.

Steps to Reproduce

1. Use the device normally after updating to HyperOS 3.0.5.0.
2. Monitor battery consumption throughout the day.
3. Compare battery backup with previous HyperOS 2.0 usage patterns.

Expected Result

Battery optimization should provide battery backup equal to or better than HyperOS 2.0.

Actual Result

Battery drains faster than expected and optimization efficiency appears reduced.

Severity

Medium

Priority

Medium

Status

Open

User Impact

Some users are expecting future updates to improve battery optimization and overall battery backup.
